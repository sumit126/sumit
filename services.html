How We Work

Our Editing Process

A seamless, transparent workflow built for studios, photographers, and couples.

01

Raw Footage Collection

Share your raw wedding footage via Google Drive, WeTransfer, or any cloud platform. We accept all camera formats including RAW and LOG files.

02

Story Planning & Music Selection

Tell us your vision, favourite moments, style preferences, and music choices. We plan the edit like a director plans a film — with intention and emotion.

03

Cinematic Editing

We craft your film with precise cuts, emotional pacing, cinematic transitions, and a narrative arc that makes every second count.

04

Color Grading & Sound Design

Professional DaVinci Resolve color grade, audio cleanup, music sync, and dialogue enhancement complete the cinematic experience.

05

Review, Refine & Final Delivery

You review the draft, we refine until it's perfect. Final delivery in HD/4K via cloud — ready to cherish and share forever.
